Off The Wall

Welcome to Off The Wall — a revolutionary workout space that breaks the mold of traditional boxing. This isn’t your average bag-punching class. Instead, you’ll be striking directly against walls that are fully lined with advanced impact-absorbing materials: ballistic rubber, foam, and gel-style padding. No gloves required. Just your energy, form, and determination.

Designed for full-body coordination and stamina, this class features more than punches and kicks. Our integrated grab bars along the base of the walls allow for intense core workouts like "crunch and punch" drills. Combined with our bounce-back flooring, every move challenges your balance and stability — engaging your core with every strike.

Off The Wall is ideal for athletes, hobbyists, and even individuals navigating neurological challenges such as Parkinson’s disease, where boxing is shown to improve motor control and mood. It’s intense, purposeful, and unlike anything you’ve experienced before.
