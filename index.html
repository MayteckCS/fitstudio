Own and Operate Your Studio

Studiofit gives you the chance to launch your own private practice — without the massive debt or long-term lease. As a part of our shared health and wellness facility, your studio operates as a fully independent business, but with all the benefits of being surrounded by professionals just like you: personal trainers, physical therapists, massage therapists, and more.

Each studio space is between 100–150 sq ft and can be customized using our interactive design tool (coming soon). For now, explore our top-down render previews to visualize your future workspace.

What's included:

  • Athletic flooring & mirror wall
  • Ballet bar, foam roller, and Bosu ball
  • Adjustable bench and block dumbbells
  • Multilevel resistance tubes, balance pad
  • Secure storage closet and personal locker space

Studios feature modern climate control and soft, bright lighting. Glass walls provide an open, airy atmosphere while maintaining privacy. Each space opens to our shared training floor with advanced equipment, giving you the best of both worlds: independence and access.
