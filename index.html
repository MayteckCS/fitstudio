Own and Operate Your Studio

Step into ownership — without the weight of debt or long-term contracts. At Fit Recruiter, we give health and fitness professionals like you a chance to launch your own private practice inside a modern, shared wellness facility. You’re not just renting a space — you're building your business alongside other top-tier specialists: personal trainers, physical therapists, massage therapists, nutritionists, and more.

Your studio, your rules. Each space ranges from 100–150 sq ft and can be tailored using our upcoming interactive design tool. Want a preview? Our top-down renderings are the perfect place to start imagining your future setup.

Picture this: You walk into your private studio outfitted with clean athletic flooring and a mirrored wall that catches every movement. A sleek ballet bar hugs one side while your foam roller and Bosu ball wait to go to work. A high-quality bench and compact dumbbell rack give you muscle without taking up space. Resistance bands, balance pads, and all the tools you need — ready to go. Your personal storage locker is tucked neatly inside a secure closet. Everything is built for performance, flow, and freedom.

And that’s just the beginning. With climate control, ambient lighting, and airy glass walls, your studio feels open yet private. Step outside, and you’re right on the edge of our shared training floor — loaded with advanced equipment and buzzing with professional energy. It’s not just where you train. It’s where you thrive.

Gym Design & Custom Branding

We don’t just rent you a space — we help you build your business. Fit Recruiter studios can be fully customized and branded with your logo, your vibe, and your layout. From signage to decor to your own website, we help you launch professionally from day one.

Our in-house team helps bring your brand to life across all channels. Whether you’re building a wellness brand from scratch or refining your existing identity, we support you with creative direction, professional signage, digital tools, and launch-ready visibility.

You’ll have control over your studio’s look and layout using our upcoming studio customization tool, with support for color schemes, layout changes, and visual branding inside and out.
